Privacy Policy for BlaBlaBla

Last updated: February 9, 2026

BlaBlaBla ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

BlaBlaBla collects the following types of information:

  • Authentication Data: When you sign in using Google, Facebook, or Apple, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo provided by these authentication providers via Firebase Authentication.
  • User ID: A unique identifier (Firebase UID) is assigned to your account for authentication and session management.
  • Room Data: Room codes, membership information, and playback status during active listening sessions.
  • Usage Data: Information about your music preferences and playback history within rooms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Authenticate users and manage user accounts
  • Create and manage shared listening rooms
  • Synchronize music playback across multiple devices in real-time
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve our service and user experience

3. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored securely:

  • User accounts: Stored in MongoDB databases and retained while your account is active
  • Room data: Automatically deleted after 24 hours of inactivity
  • Authentication data: Managed by Firebase Authentication according to Google's privacy policies

4. Third-Party Services

BlaBlaBla integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) - For secure user authentication with Google, Facebook, and Apple login
  • YouTube API (Google LLC) - For music search and video playback functionality
  • MongoDB Atlas - For secure cloud database storage

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
  • With your explicit consent

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/WSS protocols
  • Access to user data is restricted and monitored
  • Regular security assessments and updates

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Correction: Update or correct your profile information through your authentication provider
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Children's Privacy

BlaBlaBla is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

BlaBlaBla ("we", "our", or "the App") is a synchronized music listening application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use BlaBlaBla:

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Google, Facebook, Apple, or email/password, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture (if available). This information is provided by your authentication provider.
  • Room Data: When you create or join a listening room, we temporarily store the room code, member list, and playback state (current song, position, play/pause status).
  • Usage Data: We may collect basic usage information such as connection timestamps for the purpose of maintaining the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and manage your account
  • To create and manage listening rooms
  • To synchronize music playback between room members
  • To display your name/avatar to other room members

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (Google). Your credentials are stored securely by Firebase and are not accessible to us in plain text.
  • Room data is stored in MongoDB and is automatically deleted after 24 hours of inactivity (TTL).
  • We do not store your music listening history or search queries permanently.

4. Third-Party Services

BlaBlaBla uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — for user sign-in and account management. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • YouTube API (Google) — for music search and playback. Google Privacy Policy. By using the App, you also agree to the YouTube Terms of Service.
  • MongoDB Atlas — for temporary data storage. MongoDB Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is only shared with:

  • Other members in the same listening room (name and avatar only)
  • Third-party service providers listed above, solely for operating the service

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS), secure authentication tokens, and limited data retention. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

BlaBlaBla is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by signing out and discontinuing use

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
